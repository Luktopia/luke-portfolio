Luke's Real Estate Company has a long and storied tradition. It was started in 98,000 B.C. by Oog, a local caveperson living in the Chicago area. When Oog was asked, "Why call it Luke's? Who is Luke?" Oog simply stated, "One day Luke will come to lead us in the ways of real estate." To the surprise of no one, Luke did come. After his birth, almost 100,000 years after Oog's prophecy, Luke finally took control of Luke's Real Estate, the biggest real estate company in the galaxy.

So, whether you are looking to sell a gas giant orbiting Proxima Centauri or a house in Monee, Illinois, Luke's Real Estate Company is the choice for you!!!
