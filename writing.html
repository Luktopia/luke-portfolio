Shifting Brick-and-Mortar Retail Operations to the Cloud

A professional memo and white paper analyzing the outdated technological infrastructure of Times R Us, a fictional national retail chain. The paper outlines how legacy servers, inefficient inventory processes, and a lack of online presence are costing the company millions in lost productivity and missed opportunities. It recommends a full cloud migration strategy—including SaaS for communications, IaaS for data storage, cloud-based inventory systems, and a PaaS-driven mobile app—to modernize operations, reduce costs, and meet modern consumer expectations.

Lost in the Clouds? Security Trade-Offs of Cloud Migration

A professional memo evaluating the security implications of moving on-premises systems to a commercial cloud provider. Written for a fictional Chief Security Officer, the memo compares the control and isolation of on-site infrastructure with the redundancy, encryption, and advanced protections offered by modern cloud platforms. It highlights risks such as internal threat actors, outdated email systems, and single points of failure, while demonstrating how reputable cloud vendors like AWS, Azure, and Microsoft 365 provide stronger, more scalable security solutions. The memo concludes that cloud migration offers a net security benefit alongside major cost and productivity gains.
